What is a slug?

A slug is a URL-friendly version of a string, typically used in web addresses. Slugs are lowercase, with spaces replaced by hyphens or underscores, and special characters removed.

Examples:

  • Original: "Hello World!" Slug: "hello-world"
  • Original: "10 Tips & Tricks" Slug: "10-tips-tricks"
